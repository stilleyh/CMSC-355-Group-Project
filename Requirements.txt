SYSTEM REQUIREMENT SPECIFICATION

REQ1: At the hospital the Patient goes to get Registered in the system
REQ2: At the hospital the Patient enters their Personal Information and Symptoms to the system
REQ3: At the hospital the Patient goes and Moves Rooms 
REQ4: At the hospital the Patient goes and Answers Questions from doctor and nurses
REQ5: At the hospital the Patient receives an Examination from nurse and doctor
REQ6: At the hospital the Patient gets Lab Tests performed on
REQ7: At the hospital the Patient gets Monitored by doctor and nurses
REQ8: At the hospital the Patient gets Support from Doctor and Nurses
REQ9: At the hospital the Patient gets Prescribed Medication
REQ10: At the hospital the Patient gets Discharged 


REQ11: At the hospital the Doctor goes and Receives Patient’s Information from both patient and admin
REQ12: At the hospital the Doctor goes and Performs  Examination on patient
REQ13: At the hospital the Doctor observes and Takes Notes of patient’s condition
REQ14: At the hospital the Doctor goes and Sends for Lab Tests
REQ15: At the hospital the Doctor gets and Checks the Lab Results 
REQ16: At the hospital the Doctor goes and Makes Diagnosis
REQ17: At the hospital the Doctor goes and Assigns Type of Specialist Needed for patient
REQ18: At the hospital the Doctor goes and Sends Updated Patient Information to system


REQ19: At the hospital the Administration with patient’s registration and information Creates Patient Profile
REQ20: At the hospital the Administration goes and Asks Patient Questions
REQ21: At the hospital the Administration goes and Takes Vitals of patient
REQ22: At the hospital the Administration gathers and Sends Patient Information to doctor
REQ23: At the hospital the Administration goes and Receives Request to Perform Lab Test
REQ24: At the hospital the Administration goes and Performs Lab Tests on patient
REQ25: At the hospital the Administration goes and Sends Lab Results to doctor
REQ26: At the hospital the Administration goes and Receives Updated Patient Information from doctor and nurses
REQ27: At the hospital the Administration goes and Sends Prescription Information to pharmacy
REQ28: At the hospital the Administration goes and Logs All Patient Information to system 
REQ29: At the hospital the Administration goes and Discharges Patient
REQ30: At the hospital the Administration goes and Creates Hospital Bill 
REQ31: At the hospital the Administration goes and Sends Hospital Bill to patient and insurance
REQ32: At the hospital the Administration goes and Manages Rooms for staff and patients
REQ33: At the hospital the Administration goes and Manages Medicine 
REQ34: At the hospital the Administration goes and Manages Equipment
REQ35: At the hospital the Administration goes and Schedules Appointments
REQ36: At the hospital the Administration goes and Cancels Appointments
